Get a key

We have two types of keys: developer keys, which are self-service and open to all, but come with some restrictions, and commercial keys, which are based on a contract with us.

Developer key

  • Up to 25 calls per second
  • Up to 5,000 calls per day
  • Access to article text
  • Access to over 1.6m pieces of content
  • Free

Commercial key

  • Custom throttle limit
  • Custom quota limit
  • Access to article text, images, audio and videos
  • Access to entire Guardian content store
  • Contact us for costs

We use Mashery for key management. If you do not already have a Mashery account you will need to sign up for one, and you will then be forwarded to a page where you can create a new key. Otherwise log in with your existing account to create one.

If you'd like to request a commercial API key, or want to discuss commercial opportunities in general, please email us and we'll be in touch.
