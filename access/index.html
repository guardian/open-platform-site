Get Started

The open platform is a public service for accessing all the content the theguardian creates, categorised by tag and section. There are over one and a half million items available published as far back as 1999.

To get up and running, follow these simple steps:

  1. Read through our documentation
  2. Familiarise yourself with the API using our explorer
  3. Get access by registering for a key and reviewing our terms and conditions
  4. Follow us on twitter - @openplatform
  5. Ask for advice and send us your feedback using through our support forum
  6. Send us a tweet when you're app is live - @openplatform
